Binance Listing Application Form

This is the application form for token/coin listing on Binance.


Please note that the information you provide to us may be made public in the future in accordance with our data protection policy. A copy of your responses will be emailed to the address that you provide.


Before filling out this form, please read the following guidelines:
https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/binance-listing-tips-changpeng-zhao.


If you have previously filled out the application form for your project, please refrain from applying again unless there are significant updates or improvements that you would like to bring to our attention, which may be detailed in the “Project Updates” section below. Any submissions with substantially duplicative information will be DENIED.


Please be aware that we receive a large volume of applications and appreciate your patience. While we endeavor to process them as quickly as possible, we are unable to provide any direct feedback on your application if it does not pass our initial review based on the current stage your project is at.
